Tokyo Ghoul

4 Seasons

Tokyo Ghoul follows Ken Kaneki, a shy, bookish college student,
who is instantly drawn to Rize Kamishiro, an avid reader like himself. However,
Rize is not exactly who she seems, and this unfortunate meeting pushes Kaneki into
the dark depths of the ghouls' inhuman world. In a twist of fate, Kaneki is saved
by the enigmatic waitress Touka Kirishima, and thus begins his new, secret life as
a half-ghoul/half-human who must find a way to integrate into both societies.



Season & Episode Episode Name Description
S1:E1 "Tragedy" An ordinary college student named Kaneki meets a girl who also loves to read and his fate changes when she reveals herself as a ghoul who craves human flesh -- and intends to eat him alive.
S1:E2 "Incubation" When Nishiki Nishio preys on his friend Hideyoshi Nagachika, Kaneki must fight to protect him.
S1:E3 "Dove" After rescuing his friend, Kaneki is taken in by Yoshimura and Touka Kirishima.
S1:E4 "Supper" Kaneki makes friends with Tsukiyama, but Kaneki has no idea that Tsukiyama is planning a trap.
S1:E5 "Scars" Tsukiyama prepares another trap for Kaneki, using Nishio's human girlfriend as a hostage.
S1:E6 "Cloudburst" Tsukiyama is defeated by Touka, but the events have brought police attention.
S1:E7 "Captivity" Hinami's mother is killed by Mado. Touka attacks the ghoul investigation and kills Kusaba.
S1:E8 "Circular" Hinami escapes from Anteiku and is confronted by Mado.
S1:E9 "Birdcage" After the battle with Mado, Hinami moves in with Touka and Kaneki.
S1:E10 "Aogiri" Kaneki is captured by an underground ghoul faction. Amon is introduced to his eccentric new partner.
S1:E11 "High Spirits" There is a war between the CCG and Aogiri Tree, while Kaneki is being tortured by Yamori.
S1:E12 "Ghoul" Kaneki passes through several illusions, a manifestation of Rize, and starts to contemplate life.
