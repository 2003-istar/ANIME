Girls Who Code:

Girls Who Code is a program that teaches girls to code. Their main objective is to increase the amount of women in fields of Computer Science and close the gender gap in technology. The want to help shape the future female engineers.
The coders who created this site made it for a GWC Summer Immersion Program as the final project to present for graduation.
